Unstructured data for case studies¶

In addition to the continental structured and comparative data collection exercise. Narrative-based data collection was carried out for the country case studies. The aim of this was to provide country-based analysts with qualitative data that may, or may not, contribute to their own deeper knowledge of their subject matter.

Data collection was carried out using Perplexity Deep Research following a similar methodology of instructions and metadata. The metadata is recorded in the lower part of the navigation bar.

Data was collected for:

  • Data exchange
  • Digital identity
  • Digital payments
  • Institutional stakeholders
  • Individual stakeholders
