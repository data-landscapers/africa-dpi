Statistical performance metadata¶

Metadata for a selection of indicators from the World Bank Statistical Performance Indicators. 2023. Source: https://www.worldbank.org/en/programs/statistical-performance-indicators

Census¶

Population \& Housing census¶

Field Value
Variable Id stats-census-population
Definition Population censuses collect data on the size, distribution and composition of population and information on a broad range of social and economic characteristics of the population.
Format 0=Unknown or n.a.; 1=No census done within last 20 years; 2=Census done within last 20 years; 3=Census done within last 10 years
Other Metadata Original scoring: 1 Point. Population census done within last 10 years. 0.5 Points. Population census done within last 20 years. 0 Points. Otherwise
Data Collection Guidelines From World Bank Statistical Performance Indicators. Series SPI.D4.1.1.POPU

Agriculture census¶

Field Value
Variable Id stats-census-agriculture
Definition Agriculture censuses collect information on agricultural activities, such as size of holding, land tenure, land use, employment and production, and provide basic structural data and sampling frames for agricultural surveys.
Format 0=Unknown or n.a.; 1=No census done within last 20 years; 2=Census done within last 20 years; 3=Census done within last 10 years
Other Metadata Original scoring: 1 Point. census done within last 10 years. 0.5 Points. census done within last 20 years. 0 Points. Otherwise
Data Collection Guidelines From World Bank Statistical Performance Indicators. Series SPI.D4.1.2.AGRI

Survey¶

Household Survey on income, etc¶

Field Value
Variable Id stats-survey-household
Definition These surveys collect data on household income (including income in kind), consumption and expenditure.
Format 0=Unknown or n.a.; 1=No survey done within last 10 years; 2=1 survey done within past 10 years; 3=2 surveys done within past 10 years; 4=3 or more surveys done within last 10 years
Other Metadata Original scoring: 1 Point. 3 or more surveys done within past 10 years. 0.6 Points. 2 surveys done within past 10 years. 0.3 Points. 1 survey done within past 10 years. 0 Points. None within past 10 years
Data Collection Guidelines From World Bank Statistical Performance Indicators. Series SPI.D4.1.4.HOUS

Score¶

Data Use - Score¶

Field Value
Variable Id stats-score-use
Definition Indicators that capture demand side of statistical system
Format 0=Unknown or n.a.; 1=<20%; 2=20-49%;3=50-69%; 4=70-89%; 5=>89%
Other Metadata Original scoring: Weighted average of statistical performance indicators related to data services. Scores range from 0-100 with 100 representing the best score.
Data Collection Guidelines From World Bank Statistical Performance Indicators. Series SPI.INDEX.PIL1
