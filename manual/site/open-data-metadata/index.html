Open Data Inventory metadata¶

Metadata for a selection of indicators from the biennial Open Data Watch inventory (ODIN). 2024. Source: https://odin.opendatawatch.com/data

Social¶

Population and vital statistics¶

Field Value
Variable Id odin-social-pop
Definition Data evaluated across three main topics - Population, Births, Deaths.
Format 0=NA; 1=A score between 0.5 and 2.5 out of 10; 2=A score between 3 and 5 out of 10; 3=A score between 5.5 and 7.5 out of 10; a score between 8 and 10 out of 10
Other Metadata Each of the following sub-indicators are evaluated with a score 0, 0.5 or 1: Indicator coverage and disaggregation; Data available last 5 years; Data available last 10 years; Subnational level 1; Subnational level 2; Machine readability; Non-proprietary; Download options; Metadata availability; Terms of use. The value returned is the sum of these scores located in 4 score bands of 2.5 each.
Data Collection Guidelines Data and metadata from https://odin.opendatawatch.com/data
